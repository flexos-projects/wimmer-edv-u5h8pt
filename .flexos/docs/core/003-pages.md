### **Homepage**
*   **Route:** `/`
*   **Title:** Wimmer EDV | IT-Consulting für ERP & Legacy-Systeme | Schleissheim

*   **Purpose:** Establish immediate credibility and guide visitors to core expertise areas.
*   **Content Sections:**

    *   **Core Expertise Pillars:** Three distinct cards/sections for 1) Legacy System Modernization, 2) ERP Consulting, 3) Data Protection. Each links to the main service page.
    *   **Why Wimmer EDV?:** Short section on Stability, Deep Expertise, and Partnership.
    *   **Client Success Teaser:** A single, powerful pull-quote from a case study, linking to the full story.
    *   **Final CTA:** Full-width section inviting a consultation.
