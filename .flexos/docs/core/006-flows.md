These journeys map how our key target audiences will navigate the new site to find the information they need and ultimately convert into qualified leads.

### 1. Primary Journey: The Legacy System Leader (High-Intent Search)

This user knows they have a problem and is actively searching for a specialist.

*   **Entry Point:** Google search for `ibm as/400 modernisierung österreich`.
*   **Step 1: Landing Page (`/expertise/ibm-as400-iseries-consulting`)**
    *   The user lands directly on the highly relevant service page.
    *   The headline and opening paragraph immediately confirm Wimmer's expertise, building instant trust.
    *   They scan the list of specific services (RPG programming, integration, etc.), validating Wimmer's capabilities.
*   **Step 2: Deep Dive (`/kundenerfolge/case-study-as400-integration`)**
    *   The user clicks on a linked case study titled "Integration of AS/400 with modern logistics software."
    *   They read the Challenge, Solution, and Results, seeing a direct parallel to their own situation. This moves them from "interested" to "convinced."
*   **Step 3: Conversion (`/kontakt`)**
    *   Feeling confident, the user navigates to the contact page via the main navigation.
    *   They fill out the detailed "Request a Consultation" form, providing specifics about their current system and project goals because the site has earned their trust.
    *   **Action:** Form Submission.

### 2. SEO Journey: The Growth-Focused Executive (Problem-Aware Search)

This user is researching a business problem (data security) and discovers Wimmer EDV as a solution provider.

*   **Entry Point:** Google search for `veeam backup lösungen für vmware`.
*   **Step 1: Blog/Insight Article (`/insights/3-disaster-recovery-scenarios-veeam`)**
    *   User lands on a thought leadership article that clearly explains solutions to their potential problems.
    *   The article positions Wimmer EDV as knowledgeable experts, not just salespeople.
*   **Step 2: Exploration (`/expertise/datensicherung-veeam`)**
    *   The user clicks an in-article CTA: "Learn more about our Veeam services."
    *   They are taken to the main Veeam service page, where the offerings are clearly laid out.
*   **Step 3: Credibility Check (`/ueber-uns`)**
    *   Before contacting, they want to know who this company is. They navigate to the "About Us" page.
    *   The "Since 1997" story and professional presentation solidify their trust.
*   **Step 4: Conversion (Direct Call)**
    *   On their mobile device, they tap the phone number in the header to initiate a direct call.
    *   **Action:** Phone Call.

### 3. Return Visitor / Referral Journey

This user has heard of Wimmer EDV and is visiting the site directly to verify them and find contact information.

*   **Entry Point:** Direct navigation to `wimmer-edv.at`.
*   **Step 1: Homepage Scan**
    *   The user is immediately impressed by the professional design, which validates the referral.
    *   They quickly scan the core expertise pillars and partner logos, confirming the company's legitimacy.
*   **Step 2: Find Contact Info (`/kontakt`)**
    *   Their primary goal is to get in touch. They click the prominent "Kontakt" button in the main navigation.
*   **Step 3: Action**
    *   They either copy the email address or call the listed phone number.
    *   **Action:** Email or Phone Call.
