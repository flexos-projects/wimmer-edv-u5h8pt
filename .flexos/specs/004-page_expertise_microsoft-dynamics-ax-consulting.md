### Purpose
To target and convert companies running Microsoft Dynamics AX by demonstrating deep expertise in support, optimization, and migration strategies for this specific ERP.

### Components
1.  **Page Header**
    *   **Description:** A focused header with a title and an introductory statement that addresses the needs of Dynamics AX users.
2.  **Service List / Feature Blocks**
    *   **Description:** A detailed breakdown of the specific services offered related to Dynamics AX.
3.  **Value Proposition Block ("Why us for AX?")**
    *   **Description:** A section with distinct points highlighting the company's unique qualifications for Dynamics AX consulting.
4.  **Call to Action (CTA) Banner**
    *   **Description:** A prominent, page-specific CTA banner designed to capture leads for an AX-related analysis.

### Data Requirements
*   **Page Header:**
    *   `title`: "Microsoft Dynamics AX Consulting"
    *   `introduction_text`: "Maximieren Sie Ihre Investition in Dynamics AX. Wir sorgen für Stabilität, optimieren Prozesse und planen Ihre Zukunftsstrategie."
*   **Service List:**
    *   A list of service items, each with:
        *   `title`: (e.g., "Laufender Support & Wartung", "Prozessoptimierung", "Migrationsstrategie zu D365")
        *   `description`: A paragraph detailing what the service entails.
*   **Value Proposition Block:**
    *   `title`: "Warum Wimmer EDV für Ihr AX-System?"
    *   A list of key differentiators (e.g., "Langjährige Erfahrung", "Fokus auf Stabilität", "Pragmatische Lösungen").
*   **CTA Banner:**
    *   `headline`: "Ist Ihr Dynamics AX-System bereit für die Zukunft?"
    *   `cta_text`: "Unverbindliche Analyse anfordern"
    *   `cta_link`: `/kontakt`

### User Interactions
*   User clicks the CTA button to navigate to the contact page, potentially with a pre-filled interest field.

### States
*   **Default:** All content is visible. Standard hover/active states on all links and buttons.
